Project Description

Our project, "Video Image Behavior Analysis and Classification," focuses on understanding and categorizing human behavior through video footage. In this exciting endeavor, we will employ cutting-edge technology to break down complex actions and movements captured in video images, allowing us to classify and analyze them in a structured manner. Our primary goal is to create a system that can automatically recognize and classify behaviors. We will use advanced computer vision techniques to detect and interpret various actions and gestures. This could include identifying gestures like waving or nodding, recognizing activities such as walking or sitting, or even detecting emotions displayed by individuals in the video. The project will involve training machine learning models to process and classify these behaviors accurately. This technology has a wide range of applications, from enhancing security systems to tracking human movement in healthcare and entertainment.
