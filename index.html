Project Description

The "Image Classifier for Interrogation Rooms" project aims to develop a sophisticated computer program with the ability to analyze images and videos captured in interrogation rooms. This program is designed to recognize and categorize various elements, such as people's emotional expressions (e.g., happiness, sadness, anger), as well as identify any potentially suspicious activities or behaviors. The primary goal is to assist law enforcement and investigative agencies in gaining deeper insights into what transpires during interrogations. By automating the process of image and video analysis, this tool can be a valuable aid in criminal investigations, providing a more comprehensive understanding of the events within these critical rooms. Essentially, it's a smart tool that helps law enforcement personnel interpret and utilize visual information to enhance their investigative efforts.
